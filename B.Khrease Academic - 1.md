Our Vision: To accelerate the landscape of undergraduate participation in research output in Nigeria and beyond. 
Our Mission: We strive to provide the resources and technical know-how to nurture a learned skills-in-demand through networking, mentorship, and collaboration.
Our everyday duties and services @B.Khrease Academic Consult, is providing all our students with a satisfying network links to experts, mentors, and technicians for wet and dry lab procedures assistance in certified laboratories, thesis consultancy, project development and execution. We also engage in collaboration with many other organisations to train students in related skills-in-demand services at ease.

The journey highlights of B.Khrease Academic Consult
    • Founded in 2018 with a clear vision and mission that highlight the significance of expanding undergraduate research capacity in Nigeria and across continents.
    • We migrated from academic consulting to undergraduate research mentorship, our impact is measured from classrooms to research communities, and at conferences.
    • We are registered under the small and medium enterprises development agency in Nigeria (SMEDAN) in 2022.
    • We organised at least one online webinars for undergraduates annually, to position undergraduates with opportunities and information beyond the classroom before graduation.
    • We have collaborated with institutes including IBMT, CATDD and many others on research publication to propagate science finding for a sustainable future.
    • We have trained more than 1000+ undergraduates since inception, with several of them winning national and international awards from recognised organisations.
    • We have written books to support undergraduate thesis writing skills and software to explore for computer biology and bioinformatics analysis.
    • Through our founder, we have contributed as co-applicant in recognised national and international grant application drafted to address sustainable development goals (SDG) including 2, 3, 4, 12 and 17.
    • In 2025, our business name is now registered under the Cooperate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria.
    • Now, we have grown from just a classroom idea into a movement, powered by people with shared purpose, and guided by shared motive.
